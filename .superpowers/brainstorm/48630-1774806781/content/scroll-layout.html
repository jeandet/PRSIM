Scroll Area Layout Model

How should the scroll container relate to its content and the layout system?

A

Fixed viewport, virtual content

Scroll area has a fixed size in the parent layout (either explicit or flex-fill). Content is laid out in an unbounded virtual region and clipped to the viewport. Scrollbars overlay or sit alongside.

Expand-to-fit with max constraint

Scroll area grows with content up to a max size, then switches to scrolling. Like CSS max-height with overflow: auto. Good for panels that shouldn't scroll unless they have to.

Both via policy

A SizePolicy on the scroll area controls behavior: Fixed (option A), FitContent(max) (option B), or Expand (fill available space then scroll). Same scroll node, different layout hints.
